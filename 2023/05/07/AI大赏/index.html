用法：

  1. involve sth./sb. in sth.：使某事/某人参与到某事中

例句：

  • The new project will involve all departments of the company.
    （新项目将让公司的所有部门都参与其中。）
  • I don’t want to involve my family in my personal problems.
    （我不想让我的家人卷入我的个人问题中。）
  1. involve doing sth.：涉及做某事

例句：

  • The job involves working outdoors in all weather conditions.
    （这份工作涉及在各种天气条件下在户外工作。）
  • The course involves reading and writing assignments every week.
    （这门课涉及每周的阅读和写作任务。）
  1. involve oneself in sth.：参与某事

例句：

  • She always involves herself in community activities.
    （她总是参与社区活动。）
  • He decided not to involve himself in the argument.
    （他决定不卷入这场争论。）
  1. be involved in sth.：参与某事，涉及某事

例句：

  • He was involved in a car accident yesterday.
    （他昨天卷入了一起车祸。）
  • The company is involved in a legal dispute with its competitors.
    （这家公司卷入了一场与竞争对手的法律纠纷。）
  1. involve sb. with sb.：使某人与某人有关联

例句：

  • He was involved with a famous actress for several years.
    （他与一位著名女演员有过数年的关系。）
  • The police suspect that he is involved with a criminal organization.
    （警方怀疑他与一伙犯罪组织有牵连。）

词性：名词

常用中文释义：牵涉，涉及，包含

用法：

  1. involve sth.：牵涉到某事

例句：

  • The investigation revealed the involvement of several high-ranking officials.
    （调查揭示了几位高级官员的牵涉。）
  • The company denies any involvement in the scandal.
    （公司否认与这场丑闻有任何关联。）
  1. involvement in sth.：参与某事，涉及某事

例句：

  • His involvement in the project was crucial to its success.
    （他对这个项目的参与对其成功至关重要。）
  • The report highlights the country’s involvement in human rights abuses.
    （这份报告强调了该国在侵犯人权方面的涉及。）
  1. involve oneself in sth.：参与某事

例句：

  • His involvement in the charity event was greatly appreciated.
    （他对慈善活动的参与受到了高度赞赏。）
  • She regrets her involvement in the illegal activity.
    （她后悔自己卷入了非法活动中。）
